OUR OFFERING 我们的服务

Global Investor Program 全球投资者计划

An exceptional programme, the Global Investor Program ("GIP"), offers a unique financial investment and immigration scheme in Singapore. This remarkable initiative extends the privilege of legal citizenship and/or residence rights to qualified applicants in exchange for a capital investment managed by Singapore companies. 全球投资者计划（"GIP"）是一个特殊的计划，在新加坡提供独特的金融投资和移民计划。这一非凡的举措将合法公民身份和/或居留权的特权扩展到合格的申请人，以换取由新加坡公司管理的资本投资。

Tailored to the specific requirements of GIP, meticulously administered by Wealth Express, it seamlessly combines qualified investments with stable returns and dividends, and provides up to 80% of financing options. 根据GIP的具体要求量身定制，由财富快线精心管理，它将合格投资与稳定的回报和股息无缝结合，并提供高达80%的融资选择。

Transferring Capital Across Borders 跨境资本转移

Capital in many countries cannot flow freely, and many countries have laws controlling capital inflows and outflows. Under the premise of legality and compliance, we leverage our scale advantages, our large trading volume in the capital markets, our partnerships with banks and other institutions, and special channels approved by the state (such as China's QDLP and QDII) to provide solutions for our clients. 许多国家的资本不能自由流动，许多国家有控制资本流入和流出的法律。在合法合规的前提下，我们利用我们的规模优势、我们在资本市场的大量交易量、我们与银行和其他机构的合作关系，以及国家批准的特殊渠道（如中国的QDLP和QDII）为客户提供解决方案。
